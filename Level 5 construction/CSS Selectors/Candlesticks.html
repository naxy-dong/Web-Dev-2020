Candlestick holders are used to hold candles. While holding candles, the candlestick holders will have candles in them. Even while the candles are burning, the candlestick holders will still hold them. Wax may drip onto the candlestick holders but the candlestick holders will continue to hold the candles despite the wax. Candlestick holders hold candles.
