Thirty One - Rule

Start Game

  • The deck is placed in the middle of the board face down
  • The discard pile is placed besides the deck face up
  • At the start, each player is dealt 3 cards from the deck
  • The system will choose a "dealer" at random. The next player clockwise(to the dealer's left) has the first turn.

Card Values

  • The value of cards 2-10 corresponds to their face value.
  • Jack, Queen, and King all have a value of 10.
  • Ace has a value of 11.

Hand Value

  • If the cards all have different suits, the highest card value represents the score
  • Special occasion: If at least 2 cards have the same suit, adds up all of the cards of the same suit to get the score
  • Special occasion: If the hand has 3 matching cards, the score is 30.

Button Functionality

  • Click on deck: add a card from the deck to the player's hand.
  • Click on discard pile: add a card from the discard pile to the player's hand
  • Click on knock: ends the player's hand

How to Play the game

During Your Turn

  • The player may draw the top card from the deck, add it to the hand, and discard a card from the hand.
  • The player may draw the top card from the discard pile, add it to the hand, and discard a card from the hand.
  • The player may "knock", announcing that the player has a high enough score to beat everyone else's score.

Knocking

  • The knocking player may not draw a card.
  • If the player knocks, no one else may knock.
  • After a player knocks, the game continues around the table until it returns to the player who knocked.
  • The hand ends with the player who goes before the player who knocked.
  • Special occasion: As soon as a player draws a score of 31, the hand ends automatically and the round ended

Strikes

  • Everyone shows their hand and the player(s) with the lowest score receives a strike.
  • If the player who knocked and has the lowest score receives 2 strikes
  • Once a player has three strikes they are eliminated from the game.
  • The dealer shifts to the next player clockwise and begins a new round.
  • Special occasion: Every player except the player with 31 receives a strike
  • Special occasion: If the human player is eliminated, the game should continue as normal until it ends or the human player clicks the reset button.
  • Special occasion: If the last two people tie to end the game, every player (even players previously eliminated) gets back one strike, including the last two people (so everyone has 1 strike left), and the game continues.

Objective

  • To be the last player standing of the game.
